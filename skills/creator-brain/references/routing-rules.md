# Routing Rules

Use one primary module and up to two supporting modules unless the user explicitly asks for seven-expert mode.

## Commercialization

Route to `ahs` when the user asks about:

- 商业化, 变现, offer, 定价, 获客, 销售, 成交, 转化, lead generation, funnel, growth
- 产品卖不动、咨询包装、高客单价、知识付费转化、客户获取

Support with:

- `dks` when the product is creator-led, personal-brand-led, or knowledge-based.
- `lhs` when delivery, hiring, SOP, or operations are part of the bottleneck.
- `nrs` when the decision involves long-term leverage or opportunity cost.

## One-Person Company and Creator Business

Route to `dks` when the user asks about:

- 一人公司, 个人品牌, 内容系统, 创作者生意, newsletter, digital product, audience, creator monetization
- 如何发内容、如何定位、如何从内容到产品、如何搭个人 IP

Support with:

- `ahs` for monetization, offers, pricing, and sales.
- `mrs` for procrastination, confidence, or action blocks.
- `hbs` when consistency is limited by sleep, focus, stress, or energy.
- `nrs` for long-term positioning and leverage decisions.

## Organization and Execution

Route to `lhs` when the user asks about:

- 招聘, 管理, 领导力, SOP, 运营, 团队执行, 授权, 绩效, accountability
- 人多了执行差、员工不主动、流程混乱、交付不稳定

Support with:

- `ahs` when operations exist to support sales or growth.
- `nrs` when founder-level judgment or delegation tradeoffs matter.

## Wealth and Judgment

Route to `nrs` when the user asks about:

- 财富, 杠杆, 判断力, 长期主义, 方向选择, 职业选择, 机会成本, freedom
- 是否该做某个方向、服务业务还是数字产品、短期收入还是长期资产

Support with:

- `dks` for one-person leverage and digital assets.
- `ahs` for business model and revenue reality.
- `lhs` when the decision depends on team or operational complexity.

## Health and Performance

Route to `hbs` when the user asks about:

- 睡眠, 专注, 压力, 精力, 健康, 习惯, 恢复, routine, focus, stress
- 工作效率差、状态不好、注意力不稳、睡不好

Support with:

- `mrs` to turn the protocol into behavior.
- `dks` when health affects content output or creator consistency.

## Action and Behavior Change

Route to `mrs` when the user asks about:

- 拖延, 行动力, 自信, 情绪, 恐惧, 自我怀疑, motivation, confidence
- 知道该做但不做、卡住、害怕发布、无法开始

Support with:

- `hbs` when the blocker may be biological: sleep, stress, energy, nervous system.
- `dks` when the action block affects content or one-person business.
- `ahs` when the action block affects sales, outreach, or monetization.

## Trading

Route to `rts` when the user asks about:

- 交易, 技术分析, 价格行为, 趋势, 止损, 仓位, 风险管理, 回测, trading psychology

Support with:

- `nrs` for detachment, judgment, and long-term decision quality.
- `hbs` for stress, focus, and performance under pressure.

## Seven-Expert Mode

Use all seven modules only when the user asks for:

- 七人模式
- 所有知识库
- 综合 7 个专家
- full system
- complete roadmap
- 人生/事业/健康/财富整体规划

In seven-expert mode, still rank the modules by relevance. Do not give equal weight to irrelevant modules.
